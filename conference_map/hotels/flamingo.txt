Location:
The Flamingo Beach Hotel is located on the famous Mackenzie Beach (Blue Flag) where the sea is crystal clear and shallow with some of the islands best nightlife and bars.
Accommodation:
Flamingo Beach Hotel has 64 spacious, air - conditioned rooms, with side sea views.
Facilities:
The hotel has an open-air bar and patio at the entrance, a cozy lobby and a TV lounge. The private breakfast room offers a complimentary English breakfast buffet and the elegantly decorated restaurants and bars serve excellent meals, snacks, desserts and beverages.
Enjoy spectacular view of the famous Salt Lake, Airport, Larnaca City & the beautiful Blue Flag Mackenzie Beach.
